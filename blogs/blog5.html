New Rules for Handling Our Cars

By Shruti Yadav
May 8, 2022
Policy, Rules

At VehiGo, customer experience and vehicle safety are our top priorities. To ensure smoother operations and responsible usage, we've introduced new rules for handling our rental vehicles.

Whether you're a frequent renter or new to the platform, these guidelines help maintain the quality of your ride and safety for all.

üîí Updated Car Handling Rules:

  • No Smoking Policy: All vehicles are now strictly non-smoking zones.
  • Timely Returns: Late returns beyond the grace period will incur additional charges.
  • Interior Cleanliness: Customers are responsible for keeping interiors clean; excessive mess will lead to cleaning fees.
  • Unauthorized Drivers: Only the registered driver can operate the vehicle.
  • Fuel Policy: Return the car with the same fuel level as provided during pickup.

These changes were made after analyzing feedback and service patterns. Our goal is to deliver a more consistent and trustworthy rental experience.

For a complete list of rental terms and conditions, please visit our official Rental Policy Page.
