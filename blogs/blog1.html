Opening of New Offices of the Company

By Benialla Dsouza | January 14, 2022

We are thrilled to announce the grand opening of our brand-new offices in three major metropolitan cities — Mumbai, Bengaluru, and Delhi! This expansion marks a major milestone in VehiGo’s mission to make car rentals more accessible, affordable, and innovative for users across the country.

With these new facilities, we aim to enhance our logistical capabilities, improve customer service delivery, and bring our growing team closer to local communities and partners. Each new location is designed with modern workspaces, green zones, and collaborative areas to foster innovation and teamwork.

“Our team has been working tirelessly to bring this vision to life. These offices will serve as our hubs for operations, support, and development — and we're just getting started,” said VehiGo’s CEO during the launch event.

The new spaces also enable VehiGo to host more local events, build partnerships with regional car providers, and offer quicker resolution to customer queries with locally based staff.

We invite all our customers, partners, and supporters to visit our new offices and witness the future of smart car rentals.
