Statistics Show Average Car Rental Age

By Priya Deshmukh
April 15, 2022
Statistics, Industry

How old are most rental vehicles? The age of a car in a rental fleet can determine safety, comfort, and even emissions performance. Recent industry reports have revealed interesting insights.

According to the India Vehicle Rental Survey 2022:

  • ✅ 52% of rental cars are under 3 years old
  • ✅ 30% are between 3–5 years old
  • ✅ Only 18% are above 5 years old

This trend is influenced by fleet rotation policies, rising customer expectations, and government regulations. Modern cars are safer, more fuel-efficient, and equipped with better tech—making them preferable for rentals.

At VehiGo, our average rental vehicle age is just 2.4 years. We ensure our fleet remains up-to-date with the latest models, maximizing safety, comfort, and customer satisfaction.

When choosing a rental service, it's wise to ask about vehicle age and condition. It can make a huge difference in your travel experience.
