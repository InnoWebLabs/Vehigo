Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about VehiGo bookings, rentals, and services.

How can I book a car on VehiGo?
Browse available cars, choose your preferred one, and click “Book Now.” You’ll be guided through a quick and secure booking process.
What documents do I need to rent?
A valid driving license and government-issued ID (like Aadhaar or Passport) are mandatory. Payment method verification may be required.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?
Yes! You can cancel or reschedule from “My Bookings” up to 24 hours before pickup time without penalties.
Are there extra or hidden charges?
No hidden fees! All applicable taxes and charges are visible during booking. Late returns or damages may incur additional costs.
Where is VehiGo available?
VehiGo currently serves multiple major cities across India, with ongoing expansion to new regions for better access.
How can I contact support?
Visit our Contact page or email support@vehigo.com. Our team is available 24/7.
